About Catat Uangku

Catat Uangku is a daily financial tracking application designed to help everyone — from students to small business owners — record and manage their finances efficiently and simply.

Launched in 2025, this app was created to meet the need for a fast, practical, and intelligent financial solution. With features like automated receipt scanning, a comprehensive financial dashboard, and payment reminders, you can confidently manage your finances from anywhere.

We believe that well-organized finances directly impact quality of life. Catat Uangku is here to accompany you in every expense and income.

Main Features

Smart Receipt Scanner

Use your phone’s camera to upload a photo of your shopping receipt, and our system will automatically recognize and convert it into a saved transaction record.

Budgeting

Set spending limits based on categories and time periods. This helps you stay within your plan and provides real-time usage insights.

Planned Payment

Record upcoming payments like rent, loans, and subscriptions. We’ll remind you before the due date so you never miss an important payment.

Financial Dashboard

Get a complete overview of your finances in one view: transaction trends, spending categories, and usage stats in easy-to-read charts.
